Web Design Services

  • 24/7 development preview access
  • No hidden extras in quotes
02 — Hosting & Management
SERVICE 02
Website Hosting & Management

Once your website is live, it is essential to keep it up to date to ensure your customers receive the best experience. The web is constantly changing, not only the technology but also how people use it.

Our monthly website management services take the pressure and hassle out of your hands. We take care of everything: hosting, domains, backups, security, Google Search Console, Google Analytics, bug fixes, and new content.

  • UK-based web hosting
  • Daily website backups
  • Security monitoring & protection
  • WordPress & plugin updates
  • Domain & SSL management
  • Downtime monitoring
  • Bug fixes & content updates
03 — Search Engine Optimisation
SERVICE 03
Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)

Most businesses need to rank well in search results to attract the necessary traffic. SEO is the process used to enable your website to rank as well as possible on Google, Bing, and Yahoo.

Before you proceed with your site, consider what your target customers might search for. We also consider changes in technology, such as voice search and AI, which have transformed the landscape of how people search and find information online.

  • Google, Bing & Yahoo optimisation
  • Local & national SEO strategies
  • Keyword research & targeting
  • Monthly SEO reporting
  • Google Search Console setup
  • Voice search & AI readiness
  • Ongoing content optimisation
