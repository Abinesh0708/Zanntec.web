About Us

Let Go Web Help Your Business

For over a decade, we've worked with hundreds of clients throughout Yorkshire and beyond. We've helped businesses get off the ground, move forward and stay relevant.

Our Story

Built on Yorkshire grit

We specialise in building websites on WordPress. The decision was made to concentrate on this platform because it is the most widely used, well-supported and the most popular. Previously, we have also worked on other web platforms, including Magento, Drupal, Shopify and Opencart.

We have experience in front-end and back-end technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL and more. Staying up to date with what's happening in the web world is vital to us, as it ensures our skills and capabilities enable us to deliver precisely what the modern client demands.

We are particularly focused on SEO. Over the past few years, this has become a significant part of our work. We always keep up to date with what Google and the other major search engines are looking for.

Our Roots

Ethos, Approach & Values

Go Web is very proud of its Yorkshire roots. Without any funding, the business started small but grew. By consistently providing excellent service to our customers, we have built a substantial client base. We've had to do this the hard way, starting from nothing, so we understand how tough it is for new businesses to get started.

That is why we will always remain down-to-earth, to the point and realistic. We express our thoughts and take a proactive approach with our clients. We want our end product to generate revenue for you. If we disagree with you, we will inform you, provided it will benefit you and your business.

We only want the best for you and your business. Let us help you from today and into the future.
